    The Frontier Podcast: Illuvium – the bright light gaming

    Podcast

    Hear from the industry’s best and brightest about the latest news from Australia and around the world with Jonathon Miller, managing director.

    Illuvium is an open-world RPG game

    Whether you haven’t yet bought your first or want to better understand the latest or development, this show will go behind the scenes with founders, experts and industry professionals to explore the technology that powers the future of the web.

    On this episode Jonathon speaks, co-founder of Illuvium, a video game developer and publisher of AAA play-to-earn internet-games. Illuvium’s open-world exploration, creature collector and autobattler game built, is releasing on PC and Mac in 2022.

    Kieran talks to Jonathon about his sibling rivalry with his bro Kain, his love of gaming and the reasons he identified as a “no-brainer” when it comes to advancing the inherent utility of in game digital assets. We learn about how Illuvium plans to compete with AAA games, the recent “land sale”, how it makes use of layer 2 solutions to solve for scaling and the release of its companion game.

    Illuvium land:

    Sold via a Dutch Auction and utilising Immutable X’s gas-optimised minting solution, the event generated US$72m in sales – not too shabby for what most consider to be a bear market.

    The sale has been lauded as a major success by the team, and the gaming community at large. And that can be put down to the smooth user experience and total avoidance of the FOMO-induced purchasing-fee hikes commonly associated with popular events.
